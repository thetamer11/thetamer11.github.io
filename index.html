Hey there, I’m Gavin!

Ever since I was young, I’ve been entranced by games and how they work. I would spend countless hours researching, listening to talks, and designing games that I could make in the future. Everything that I did had to lean into my game development career, no matter if it was classes or extracurriculars.

In 2016 I finally started to develop my own games, they were small and short, but it was a start. Since then I’ve participated in numerous game jams along with getting involved in my local IGDA chapter.

As an Engineer and Game Developer I love challenging myself and learning something new, whether that be new tools, techniques, or languages. This has helped me quickly pick up new concepts and further optimize my code.
